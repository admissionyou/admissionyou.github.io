About Us

Regina Kilner
MA, Professional Writing and Editing
BFA, Creative Writing

Regina has been tutoring students in English, Writing, and English Literature for over 15 years and taught Writing Composition courses at Northern Virginia Community College for 5 years. She is a freelance writer who has written for the McGraw-Hill/Glencoe middle school to high school blog "BTW Stuff You Should Know." In the past 10 years she has assisted high school students with their college selection, and with successful essay ideas and composition.


Leslie Mustain
MPA, Public Administration
BS, Business Administration

Leslie spent over 10 years working for the Executive Office of the President analyzing and developing programs to support secondary and higher education. She helped craft the President's budget and grant proposals for education programs and worked extensively on developing new programs to advance higher education opportunities and access for all students. She is a wealth of knowledge on both private and public universities around the country. In the past few years, she has assisted local students with college selection as well as successful essay ideas and composition.
